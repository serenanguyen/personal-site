About Me

I'm from Houston, Texas but moved to Austin for school. I graduated from the University of Texas with a biology degree with a concentration in evolution, ecology, and behaviors.

In my free time I volunteer at a wildlife rehab where I get to take care of injured animals until they can be released. I also like to lift and boulder.

While working in operations at a tech company I became interested in becoming a QA. I began learning QA automation which got me interested in coding. I worked through online modules to learn basic html, css, javascript. I never considered a career in developing but it has been exciting for me to create things. I have enrolled in the UT Coding Bootcamp hoping to become a fullstack developer.

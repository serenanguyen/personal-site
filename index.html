About Me

I love building and designing visually pleasing, user-friendly applications. My learning style for coding is similiar to how I assemble Ikea furniture, just tinker until the pieces fit together and maybe look at the instruction booklet at some point. It's exciting to see how many different ways there are to solve one problem. It just means there's always something more to learn. When I'm not coding you can find me swimming and climbing at the greenbelt, crafting a new home for my succulents, or rehabilitating injured and abandoned wildlife.

About Me

I'm from Houston, Texas but moved to Austin for school. I graduated from the University of Texas with a biology degree with a concentration in evolution, ecology, and behaviors.

In my free time I volunteer at a wildlife rehab where I get to take care of injured animals until they can be released. I also like to lift and climb.

While working in operations at a tech company I became interested in becoming a QA. I began learning QA automation which got me interested in coding. I worked through online modules to learn basic html, css, javascript. It has been extremely exciting for me to learn material completely new to me as well as create exciting projects. I am currently a student at the UT Coding BootCamp with the intention of becoming a full-stack developer by the end of May 2017.
